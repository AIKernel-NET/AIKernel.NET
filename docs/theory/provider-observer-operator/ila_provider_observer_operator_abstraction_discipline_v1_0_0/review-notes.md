# Review Notes

## Summary

This package prepares the Provider–Observer–Operator supplement to Interface-Led Architecture (ILA) for Zenodo publication.

## Main editorial decisions

- The English manuscript is treated as the canonical version.
- The Japanese manuscript is included as a companion translation.
- The paper is positioned as a supplement to the published ILA paper, DOI: 10.5281/zenodo.20290614.
- The role model is stated as an interface-extraction discipline, not as an ontological claim about all software entities.
- Semantic IR and Composite Distance are explicitly separated from the general ILA theory and treated as AIKernel specializations.
- Exceptions are defined as failure states of Pipeline or Contract transitions, not as roles.
- Reactive architecture is discussed as a related perspective and generalized rather than criticized.
